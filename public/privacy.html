Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

MenoMind ("we," "us," or "our") operates menomind.app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Email address — when you submit the symptom assessment quiz, sign up for our email series, or contact us
  • Quiz responses — your answers to the perimenopause symptom assessment, including symptom categories and severity scores
  • Payment information — processed securely by Stripe; we never see or store your full card number

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, click patterns, and scroll depth via Google Analytics 4
  • Device information — browser type, operating system, screen resolution, and IP address (anonymized)
  • Cookies and tracking pixels — used by Google Analytics, Google Ads, and Meta Pixel for analytics and advertising purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver your personalized symptom assessment results
  • To send your email nurture series (educational content about perimenopause)
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe
  • To improve our website and services through analytics
  • To serve relevant advertising through Google Ads and Meta
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Supabase — database hosting and authentication (data stored in the US)
  • Stripe — payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Resend — email delivery service
  • Google Analytics 4 — website analytics (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google Ads — advertising and conversion tracking
  • Meta Pixel — advertising and conversion tracking (Meta Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel — website hosting

4. Data Retention

We retain your email address and quiz results for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us at support@menomind.app.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing emails (via unsubscribe link in every email)
  • Opt out of tracking cookies (via your browser settings)

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential functionality — remembering your quiz progress and session state
  • Analytics — understanding how visitors use our site (Google Analytics 4)
  • Advertising — measuring ad effectiveness and building remarketing audiences (Google Ads, Meta Pixel)

You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some site functionality may be affected.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

MenoMind is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, contact us at:

support@menomind.app
